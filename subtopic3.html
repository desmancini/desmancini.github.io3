How Technology Affects Child Development
Site Author
 Florida Tech


Abstract
 This website provides information on how technology affects children and their development. It gives examples of positive and negative effects. The examples of the negative effects are technology harms attention spans, reduces self soothing and self regulation, increases aggression, stagnates physical activity, has a negative effect on school performance, limits interpersonal interactions, and negatively affects emotional development. The positive effects are that technology helps visual spatial, multi tasking, and aids learning. Along with listing these positive and negative effects, this website further explains what these effects do to a child and how technology contributes to the development of these issues or benefits.

 Impact of Technology on Children
Site Author
 WGU
Abstract
 This website is similar to the last in providing information on the positive and negative effects of technology on children and their development. The negative effects that the article includes are lowering attention spans, lack of privacy, risk of depression and poor mental health, obesity, poor grades in school, bullying, and social interaction issues. The positive effects that were listed are that technology is a good tool for classrooms and learning, preparing for future careers in technology, improved multitasking, visual spatial development, problem solving, and decision making. This website also provides further information on each of these effects and sources where more information can be found.
