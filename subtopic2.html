Tips for Parents Regarding Technology and Children
Site Author
 Erikson Institute


Abstract
 This article provides information on technology and the use of it at home and in family life. It also provides a handful of statistics relating to the use of technology by children under the age of six, how much time parents spend using technology with their young children, the percentage of different concerns reported by parents about children under six using technology, and more. The most significant part of this article for this research is a section where the author provides tips for parents to use when they plan to use technology with their young children. Some of these tips include, parents should explore technology with their children, manage screen time, have a healthy balance of technology use, and more.

 Tips for Teachers When Using Technology in a Preschool Classroom
Site Author
 Missy Knechel
Abstract
 This website provides helpful information for teachers to keep in mind when incorporating technology in their preschool classrooms. Important questions for teachers to keep in mind are what type of technology is appropriate to use in the classroom, how often young children should spend on technology, and how can teachers have a healthy balance in using technology. The tips that the article provides are to have a plan when using technology with preschool children, keep the activities active and hands on to produce excitement, practice safe use of technology, use a diverse group of devices and platforms for learning, and use technology to engage with parents and staff members.
